Privacy Policy

Data Collection

The Screenplay Writer Add-on does not collect any personal information or data from its users. The Add-on strictly functions as a tool to assist with formatting documents to screenplay standards within Google Docs. New Cinematics does not have access to any information related to the content or metadata of the documents you create or modify using this Add-on.

Data Usage

Since no data is collected by the Screenplay Writer Add-on, we do not use any personal or document-related information in any capacity. Your work remains entirely private and cannot be accessed by New Cinematics or any third parties.

Data Storage

The Add-on does not store any user data. All data related to your documents remains in your Google Docs environment and is subject to Google’s privacy policies. The developers of Screenplay Writer do not have access to this data and thus cannot store it.

Contact Details

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at: betashorts1998@gmail.com

Cookies and Tracking

The Screenplay Writer Add-on does not use any cookies, log files, or tracking mechanisms. We do not monitor or track any user activity while using the Add-on.

Opting Out

Since the Add-on does not collect any data, there is no data collection or usage to opt out from. You may discontinue using the Add-on at any time by removing it from your Google Docs account.

Third-Party Services

This Add-on does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. Any interaction with external services (e.g., through Google Docs) is governed by the respective third-party’s privacy policy. Screenplay Writer disclaims any responsibility for the privacy practices of such services.

Google Docs Data

Google Docs does not use content created within its environment for advertising purposes. This Add-on operates entirely within Google Docs, and all document content is governed by Google’s privacy policies. The Screenplay Writer Add-on does not interact with this data beyond assisting with formatting tasks.
